Why this summit

AI must become sustainable before it becomes universal.

The global AI race is often defined by larger models, larger data centers and larger compute bills. India needs a different imagination: AI that preserves languages, reduces unnecessary compute, serves local public needs and builds responsible models around Indian Knowledge Systems without costing the earth.

Four pillars

The architecture of Sustainable Intelligence

Core theme

Indian Knowledge Systems for AI

How can AI models be designed with Indian Knowledge Systems in mind — not as an afterthought, but as a foundation for culturally grounded, linguistically rich and context-aware intelligence?

Minimum Viable Intelligence

A practical discussion on when a compact 3B-parameter model can serve people better than a massive cloud-dependent model.

Community-Led Data

A grounded roadmap for language data, domain data and Indian Knowledge Systems that are ethically sourced, carefully structured and publicly useful.

The Flagship Syllabus

Explore the detailed 18-hour curriculum designed to systematically upgrade your legal workflows.

1
The AI Paradigm Shift & The Lawyer's Desk

Focus: Understand AI without technical jargon. Learn the difference between General AI (ChatGPT) and Legal-Native AI (Harvey, CoCounsel). Navigate confidentiality and the "Human-in-the-Loop" workflow.

🛠️ LAB 1: "Red-Teaming" the AI: Deliberately prompt a general LLM to hallucinate a fake legal precedent. Spot the visual/structural "tells". Apply the Traffic Light Framework to safe/unsafe delegation.

Deliverable: AI Use Policy for your firm.

2
The Art of Legal Prompt Engineering

Focus: Stop searching, start commanding. Move beyond basic questions to structured prompt frameworks (Role + Task + Jurisdiction + Facts + Format + Constraints).

🛠️ LAB 2: The 5-Minute Cease & Desist: Input a messy, bulleted list of client grievances to generate a polished C&D letter. Iterate the prompt for three tones: Highly Aggressive, Firm but Amicable, and Legally Neutral.

Deliverable: 20-Prompt Starter Kit.

3
Supercharged Legal Research & Case Understanding

Focus: Accelerate research preparation while preserving rigor. Frame research questions, extract holdings, and distinguish ratio vs. obiter.

🛠️ LAB 3: The Research Triage: Convert a client problem into a research plan. Summarize a judgment in three formats (partner brief, detailed note, client explanation). Verify claims against source material.

Deliverable: Case brief sheet & Verification Checklist.

4
AI for Drafting: Notices, Memos & Opinions

Focus: Produce better first drafts faster. Draft from facts vs. precedent, and instruct AI to avoid overclaiming.

🛠️ LAB 4: The Strategy Synthesizer: Given a complex employment dispute fact pattern, generate the top 5 legal vulnerabilities, draft a memo of opposing counsel's arguments, and shrink a long opinion into a 1-page executive memo.

Deliverable: Drafting prompt pack & Legal memo skeleton.

5
Accelerating Document Review

Focus: Turn a 5-hour review into a 15-minute verification task. Digest massive files, extract clauses, and spot risks.

🛠️ LAB 5: The "Needle in the Haystack" Audit: Compare two versions of a 50-page lease. Extract indemnification clauses, generate a red-flag list, identify missing standard protections, and prepare fallback language.

Deliverable: Contract review checklist & Clause deviation table.

6
Fact Management & E-Discovery

Focus: Turn document chaos into litigation-ready structure. Extract facts from messy emails, notices, and transcripts.

🛠️ LAB 6: The Evidence Matrix: Upload a mixed bundle of facts to create a master chronology, an actor map, an issue-to-evidence table, and a witness question bank.

Deliverable: Chronology builder prompt & Evidence matrix template.

7
Client Communication & Simplification

Focus: Reduce friction with clients while preserving legal accuracy. Translate dense jargon into plain language for founders and boards.

🛠️ LAB 7: The Client-Facing Translator: Take a dense Supreme Court ruling and generate an internal executive summary, a polite 3-paragraph email to a non-lawyer client, and a compliant LinkedIn post.

Deliverable: Client explanation sheet & FAQ prompt bank.

8
Compliance, Policy, and Legal Ops

Focus: Reclaim your day from administrative friction. Automate client intakes, policy summaries, and standard operating procedures (SOPs).

🛠️ LAB 8: The Ops Automator: Extract obligations from a regulation text to build a compliance tracker. Design a no-code intake workflow for legal requests.

Deliverable: Obligation extraction sheet & SOP drafting template.

9
Responsible AI Practice & Ethics

Focus: Build professional judgment. Address hallucinations, false completeness, anonymization, and professional accountability.

🛠️ LAB 9: The Repair & Rescue: Diagnose a deliberately bad AI-generated legal memo with fake citations. Rewrite prompts to force uncertainty disclosure and source discipline.

Deliverable: Safe-use protocol & Confidentiality screening checklist.

Capstone: The "Friday Afternoon Emergency"

A realistic, high-pressure simulation bringing together all skills learned.

The Brief (4:00 PM)

A client sends a panicked email with a messy narrative and a 30-page opposing contract attached.

Required Outputs (by 4:45 PM)

  • Summarize core issue & flag contract risks
  • Draft holding response to opposing counsel
  • Draft actionable strategy email to client
  • Manual verification risk note

Facilitator notes

Practical setup guidance for running the one-day lab smoothly.

One-day intensive — delivery checklist
  • Staffing: 1 lead instructor + 1 legal co-facilitator for verification practice + 1 tech runner (AV, accounts, Wi‑Fi).
  • Materials: Provide a shared “master case file” (contracts + emails + short judgment excerpt) and a template pack (research plan, memo skeleton, clause review checklist, chronology/evidence matrix).
  • Safety protocol: Start with confidentiality rules, anonymisation guidance, and a “no client data” policy for live prompting. Emphasise source-checking and citation/fact verification.
  • Room setup: Strong Wi‑Fi, power strips, and small-group seating (3–5 per group) for rapid peer-review cycles.
  • Hands-on pacing: Keep sessions tight: demo (5–8 min) → guided practice (20–30 min) → critique (10 min) → reusable workflow capture (5 min).
  • Capstone: Use the “Friday Afternoon Emergency” simulation to combine drafting + review + verification under time pressure; collect outputs as take-home templates.

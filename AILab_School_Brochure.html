Our philosophy
Right-size intelligence — the right AI for the right task
Most AI platforms send every query to the largest, most expensive model available. AILab takes a different approach: our workbench matches model capability to task complexity. A Class 4 student sorting patterns does not need the same compute as a Class 11 student debugging Python. This keeps costs efficient, data exposure minimal — and critically, gives Indian-built models a meaningful role in your school's AI experience.

Safety & data protection

No student data ever reaches an AI model

🔒 PII blocked at source

Aadhaar numbers, phone numbers, addresses — filtered out at the platform layer before any prompt reaches any model. This protection applies across every AI vendor in the workbench.

📋 DPDP Act 2023

Written parental consent before any student account is activated. A dedicated Data Privacy Officer manages compliance, consent records, and incident response throughout.

👁️ Full audit trail

All sessions are logged for 12 months. Your principal has read-only access to the audit dashboard. GreenAI accesses logs only with your school's written approval.

📊 Predictable usage

Token quotas are set per school and per student. No student can generate surprise costs. Quotas are reviewed and adjusted transparently — never billed retrospectively.

AILab's workbench uses best-in-class AI models from multiple vendors. Our protection guarantee is not about where models are hosted — it is about what data they ever see. The answer is: nothing that identifies your students.
